About Teliex

One team, five disciplines, zero handoff friction.

Founded in Ondo State and now serving clients across Nigeria, Teliex Technologies exists because businesses shouldn't need five different vendors to keep their tech running.

Our Story

Started with a repair bench. Grew into a full tech partner.

Teliex began as a phone repair bench solving a simple, recurring problem: getting a device fixed fast, without the runaround. As clients kept asking for more — a logo here, a website there, a bot to answer customer questions — we built out the team to match.

Today, the same people who diagnose a cracked screen also ship production websites and train AI systems. That closeness between hardware and software is still our biggest advantage.
