FAQ

Quick answers before you reach out.

How fast is a typical phone repair?

Most screen, battery, and port repairs are same-day. Water damage or parts-on-order jobs may take 2–4 days — we'll tell you upfront during diagnostics.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs and builds?

Yes. Repairs carry a 30-day parts warranty, and web/software builds include a post-launch support window — details are confirmed in your quote.

Can you combine services, like a website plus a chatbot?

That's actually where we're strongest — one team handling both means no lost context between the website and the AI layer on top of it.

Do you work with clients outside Ondo State?

Yes — design, web development, AI solutions, and remote IT support are all delivered to clients across Nigeria. Phone repair is in-person at our bench.
